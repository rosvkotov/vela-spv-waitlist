What Vela does

An AI ops team for your syndicate.

The operational layer behind every deal — so a one- or two-person operation runs like an institutional one.

AI-powered investor communication

Vela's AI handles investor comms for you — personalised chasing through KYC, signing and payment, and instant answers to investor questions around the clock. Every message is grounded in that specific deal's terms and documents, so investors get fast, accurate responses and rounds close on time.

AI-drafted investor memos

Upload the pitch deck and Vela drafts a structured investment memo in minutes — company overview, round terms, key risks and funding history. You review and refine instead of writing from a blank page, so memos go out faster and read consistently across every deal.

Onboarding & reporting, automated

Investors complete eligibility certification, KYC and e-signing in minutes through a guided flow. After close, quarterly updates are drafted automatically from live portfolio data and EIS/SEIS tax certificates are tracked and distributed — institutional-quality reporting without the manual work.

Banking built in

Every deal gets a dedicated, ring-fenced bank account, opened automatically. Investors wire in, funds release to the company on close, and distributions go back out — multi-currency and client-money protected, with none of the legacy-bank setup.
